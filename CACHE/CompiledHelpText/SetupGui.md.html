Language Selector Help

NLTK is required for finding synonyms to words and this program relies on it to give predictions for synonyms you may want to add.

If you are running into crashes, re downloading NLTK may help

Languages are stored in /Data/language_name.db The only allowed characters in language names are alphanumerics, '-' and '_'

Two languages aren't permitted to have the same name

Deleting a language

To prevent accidental deletion of a language, languages can't be deleted from inside the app. To delete a language go to the Data folder in the root of where this app is stored and delete language_name.db
